The Baseline Reality

Heavy Infrastructure Dependency

Standard GenAI stacks often rely on GPU-backed FP16 models that require several GBs of VRAM for practical inference.

Latency Bottlenecks

Cloud-based APIs introduce network latency and cold-start overheads. Local FP32 models on CPU are slow and inefficient.

Data Privacy Constraints

Sending proprietary documents to external embedding services or LLM APIs introduces privacy and compliance risks.
